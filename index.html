About

I am a PhD student interested in speech perception, adaptation, Bayesian modeling, and the cognitive mechanisms that allow people to adjust to variation in language.

Research

My work asks how listeners adapt to new talkers, accents, and speech distributions. I am especially interested in the boundary conditions of rationality: when human adaptation approximates ideal inference, and when it departs from it.
